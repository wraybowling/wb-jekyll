I set out to create my first piece of hardware; a digital video synthesizer for Eurorack systems.

Video Synthesis Primer

Video synths are largely analog. They employ a method of processing video signals by treating the electron signal as a single waveform or a combination of waveforms that are synchronized to the scanlines of a CRT. LZX Industries is at the forefront of producing video synthesis hardware that is available to the public, but the pricepoint is well out of reach for even the spendiest curious person.
