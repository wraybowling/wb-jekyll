Star Wars Uncut was a collaborative project that I found through Vimeo. I chose my favorite scene from Star Wars — just as Rebel forces are leaving Yavin-4 to attack the Death Star, a squadron of X-Wings floats up above a lush sea of tree-tops and slowly float off in to the sunset. It also includes one of the pivotal lines in the film where Obi-Wan tells luke "The Force will be with you" — Star Wars Uncut's surrogate title for this fifteen second clip.

Process

Models were pieced together from flat digital paintings. Each shot was then played back through the Blender Game Engine, and recorded in realtime.
