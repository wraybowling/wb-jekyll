Changing Wallpapers

The part about spending my summer programming and changing wallpapers on my desktop is profoundly true. Two frames in the animation feature two actual wallpapers that I rendered for a pair of monitors that were of slightly different sizes. Creating and then aligning each wallpaper to the monitors took longer than the rest of the stop motion animation combined — partially because I was using Indigo Renderer. There's a time-lapse if you want to see it.
